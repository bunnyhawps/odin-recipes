The Best Basic Mashed Potatoes

Why You Should Make This

What kind of steak meal does NOT sound good with some good ol' mashed potatoes? This basic, yet buttery and smooth mashed potatoes will be the perfect partner for your juicy and delicious steak!

Ingredients

  • 2 lb baking potatoes, peeled, and quartered
  • 3 cloves garlic, peeled, or to taste (Optional)
    • <1i>1 c milk
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil on high heat. Throw in the potatoes and garlic, lower heat to medium, and simmer until potatoes are tender(15-20 min). Just chill in the meantime.
  2. When the potatoes are almost finished, heat milk and butter in a small saucepan over low heat until butter is melted. Make sure you time it right or you're gonna have some overcooked potatoes or undercooked and they're gonna be too hard.
  3. Drain the potatoes and return to the pot. Slowly add the warm milk mixture, blending it in with a potato masher or electric mixer until potatoes are smooooth and creamy af. Season with salt and pepper and you're done!
